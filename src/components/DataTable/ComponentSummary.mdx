##Summary
The datatable is a structured presentation of data in rows and columns, often used for organizing and displaying tabular information. It enables efficient data analysis, sorting, and comparison, for visualizing and managing datasets in a structured format.

##Purpose
The datatable is used for streamlining data presentation, enabling users to make informed decisions, perform data-driven tasks, and gain insights from large datasets with ease.
